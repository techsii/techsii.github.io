About Us

The online Complain System provides users with a convenient platform to report incidents to law enforcement agencies. Users can submit detailed complaints, including personal information and specifics about the incident, such as its type and location. The system's integration of Aadhaar verification adds an extra layer of security and authenticity to the complaints filed. Additionally, users can categorize the incident accurately and provide detailed descriptions, aiding law enforcement in understanding the situation. The option to attach relevant files,images & video further enhances the reporting process. With the potential for automated email notifications, users can stay informed about the status of their complaint, ensuring transparency and efficiency in handling cases.

Complaint Filing: Guide

This user manual serves as a concise guide for efficiently filing complaints through our online system. Firstly, users are instructed to log in to their account on the website. Upon logging in, they navigate to the designated "File Complaint" section. Here, they complete a form with accurate details regarding the incident and attach any necessary supporting documents. After reviewing the entered information for accuracy, users simply click the "Submit" button to finalize the complaint submission. Additionally, users can manage their account details, such as updating personal information or changing passwords, as needed. Finally, for any inquiries or assistance during the process, users are encouraged to reach out to our support team.
